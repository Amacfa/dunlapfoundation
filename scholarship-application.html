Program Details

Scholarship Overview

The Evelyn Dunlap Foundation Scholarship supports deserving students in their educational journey, providing financial assistance and mentorship to help them achieve their academic goals.

Scholarship Overview

The Evelyn Dunlap Foundation Scholarship supports students who demonstrate academic excellence, leadership potential, and community engagement. Our selection process identifies candidates poised to create meaningful impact while pursuing their education.

Key Requirements

  • Academic: 3.8+ GPA with full-time enrollment at an accredited institution
  • Leadership: Demonstrated leadership experience and community involvement
  • Vision: Clear academic goals that align with our foundation's mission

Application Package

  • Completed application form and official transcripts
  • Resume and two recommendation letters
  • Personal statement and impact essay

Application Process

1

Submit Your Application

Fill out the complete application form below with all required personal and academic information.

2

Initial Review

Our scholarship committee will evaluate all applications based on academic achievement, leadership potential, and alignment with our mission.

3

Finalist Selection

Selected finalists may be contacted for additional information or interviews.

4

Award Notification

All applicants will be notified of their status by December 1, 2025. Recipients must attend the award ceremony on January 3, 2026.

Award Details

Award Amount

$5,000 to $10,000 per recipient

Funds can be used for tuition, educational resources, and essential living expenses. Recipients must maintain academic standards, participate in foundation events, and serve as ambassadors for the foundation.

Important

Recipients must attend the award ceremony on January 3, 2026. The foundation may request documentation verifying educational use of funds.
