Evelyn Dunlap · 1927–2023
02 The Founder

A foundation in faith & service.

I Mission

The Evelyn Dunlap Foundation is dedicated to empowering individuals through accessible education and fighting hunger in our communities. We believe education is the cornerstone of progress — and that no one should have to choose between learning and basic needs.

Through scholarship programs, community initiatives, and partnerships with leading institutions, we work to build a more equitable future where every person can pursue their full potential.

II History

Founded after Evelyn's passing on January 4, 2023, the foundation emerged from her son JonPierre Carlo's commitment to continuing her legacy of faith, service, and love for others. What began as a tribute has grown into an organization that spans scholarships, partnerships, and community work.

Our journey is inspired by Evelyn's own — born one of twelve children, she overcame extraordinary obstacles to create a loving home where education and dignity were paramount.

III Inspiration

Evelyn Dunlap (1927–2023) was a woman of profound faith and unwavering strength who raised six children as a single mother while serving her church and community. Her life embodied the principles of compassion, dignity, and service that continue to guide the foundation today.
