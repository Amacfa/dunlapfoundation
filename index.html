About Evelyn Dunlap Foundation

Learn about our mission, history, and the inspiration behind our commitment to creating positive change through education and community support.

Our Mission

The Evelyn Dunlap Foundation is dedicated to empowering individuals through accessible education and fighting hunger in our communities. We believe that education is the cornerstone of progress and that no one should have to choose between education and basic needs.

Through our scholarship programs, community initiatives, and partnerships with leading institutions, we're committed to building a more equitable future where everyone has the chance to achieve their full potential.

Our History

Founded in honor of Evelyn Dunlap, our foundation emerged from a deep commitment to continuing her legacy of elegance, grace, and service to others. What began as a small initiative has grown into a comprehensive organization that spans multiple programs and partnerships across the country.

Our journey has been marked by meaningful collaborations, impactful projects, and the unwavering support of donors who share our vision for a better world.

Our Inspiration

Evelyn Dunlap embodied the timeless elegance and philanthropic spirit reminiscent of figures like Jackie Kennedy and Sophia Loren. Her approach to life combined classic values with a forward-thinking mindset that continues to inspire our work today.

This philosophy guides everything we do—from the design of our programs to the way we engage with our communities and partners, always maintaining her standard of excellence and compassion.

Educational Opportunities

Scholarship Program

Our scholarship program supports deserving students in their educational journey, providing financial assistance and mentorship to help them achieve their academic goals.

Scholarship Guidelines

Our scholarship program is designed to support students who demonstrate academic excellence, leadership potential, and community involvement. Applicants are evaluated based on a holistic review of their achievements and aspirations.

  • Must be enrolled in an accredited university or college
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0 on a 4.0 scale
  • Demonstrated leadership qualities and community involvement
  • Financial need is considered in the selection process
  • Commitment to academic excellence and personal growth

Award Details

Scholarship amounts range from $2,500 to $10,000 based on merit and need. Funds are disbursed directly to the educational institution and can be used for tuition, books, and other academic expenses.

Recipients also gain access to our mentorship program, connecting them with professionals in their field of study for guidance and support.
